My top 6 gourmet

Shiroi Koibito(Hokkaidou)

This is one of the best gifts to bring back home from Hokkaidou. This cookie has white chocolate sandwiched between the two pieces made up of cokkie dough.

Kiritanpo(Akita)

This delicious food made up of mochi rice, can go well with anykind of dish and often eaten during winter.

Monjya(Tokyo)

This food is similar to the okonomiyaki originated in Oosaka, but has Tokyo's original flavor in it. Monjya can be eaten with multiple people all chatting and surrounding the table.

Houtou(Yamanashi)

Houtou has a special kind of noodles which makes it stand out from the other noodle dishes. It also has lots of healthy vegetable inside, so customers can enjoy the noodles unique taste as well as the vegetables.

Okonomiyaki

Okonomiyaki is one of the famous Japanese food next to Sushi. It is originally from the Kansai area mainly from Osaka. Many people can gather around and eat this delicious gourmet while talking amongst their families or friends.

Taco Rice

Taco Rice was probably told by American citizens who used to live in Okinawa and has continued to spread around Japan. It is the perfect main dish for any kind of dish because it had rice and salad combined. This healthy gourmet is made from different spices all around the world to show the culture of each country.

About Me

I have lived in the United States of America for a little more than three years when I was in elementary school. During my time abroad, I've learned about the different cultures in America and since America is a very diverse country, even had the opportunity to experience them myself. I've also learned that Japanese food is well known all around the world, so I decided to make a display of most of the famous gourmet in Japan that many tourists crave for during their stay in Japan.
